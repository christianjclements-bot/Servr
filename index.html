Everything you need to know

Download the Servr app, log in with the email you registered with, and tap "File a claim." You'll be asked to describe the issue and upload a short video or photo. We'll respond within 24 hours with next steps.
Your Servr warranty covers you for 2 years from your original date of purchase. The coverage period starts the day you bought the product, not the day you registered.
We cover 40+ countries and are expanding continuously. If a local technician isn't available in your specific area, we'll arrange depot repair (we cover shipping both ways) or a full replacement at no extra cost to you.
Standard Servr plans cover mechanical and electrical failure — not accidental damage. If you dropped it, spilled on it, or it was physically damaged, that falls outside standard coverage. Check your plan documents for full exclusions.
